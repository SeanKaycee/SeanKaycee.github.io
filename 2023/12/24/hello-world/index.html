How to Apply LLMs in Finance

Created2023-12-24|Updated2023-12-26
|Post Views:

  Recent advances in large language models (LLMs) have opened new possibilities for artificial intelligen-ce applications. We can get lots of LLMS by using some finetuning methods on foundation models, and then use them in specific domains.

Here are some cases

Finance

One can use LLMs for free finance help, and get some advices of inform trading, risk modeling, customer service, and more. Moreover, LLMs can extract unstructured information from text language, which can effectively improve the trading efficiency of financial markets.

Text generation

LLMs can generate formatted text very fast. Say, If you are writting an offical email to inform others information of an academic conferences, and you don’t know how to start.. You can ask LLMs to write a template for you. Despite of emails and reports, you can also ask LLMs to write a short nolve with specific style. Maybe the generated texts are not so good for some complex tasks, but they can still inspire you in some ways.
